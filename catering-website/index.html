— FAQ

A few good questions.

How far in advance should we book?
Weddings: 6–12 months. Corporate & private dining: 3–6 weeks ideal, but we keep one rapid-response slot open each week for last-minute requests.
Do you handle venues without a kitchen?
Yes — barns, lofts, rooftops, gardens, beaches. We build a full mobile kitchen on site and bring everything down to the salt shaker.
How do you handle dietary requirements?
We design every menu with parallel plant-based, gluten-free and allergen-aware versions. No guest gets the "boring" plate.
Do you travel?
All over. We've catered events from Mexico City to Marrakesh. Travel & lodging billed at cost.
What does a typical proposal include?
A bespoke menu, drinks list, staffing breakdown, rental list, timeline, and a single transparent number with no surprise line items.
